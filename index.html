Facts & Nutrition

Botanical Family

Not a potato! Sweet potatoes (Ipomoea batatas) belong to the morning glory family, while regular potatoes are in the nightshade family.

Vitamin A Powerhouse

Orange-fleshed varieties are packed with beta-carotene, which your body converts to Vitamin A‚Äîessential for vision and immunity.

Color Varieties

They come in a spectrum of colors: orange, white, red, yellow, and purple. Purple varieties are high in anthocyanins.

Popular Recipes

Roasted Sweet Potato Fries

A healthy, crispy alternative to regular fries. Tossed with a little oil, salt, and paprika, then roasted at high heat.

  • 2 large Sweet Potatoes
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Paprika

Sweet Potato & Black Bean Chili

A hearty, vegan dish that balances the potato's sweetness with savory spices and beans.

  • 1 Sweet Potato, cubed
  • 1 can Black Beans
  • Diced tomatoes & spices
